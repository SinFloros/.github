# Sin Floros 🇵🇪

Sin Floros is a civic-tech organization focused on public information that is
clear, verified, and useful. This profile gives you a concise view of our
vision, mission, and partnership approach.

## Vision 🔭

We envision an informed electorate that can make confident decisions without
wasting hours searching for reliable sources.

## Mission 🎯

We build trustworthy, accessible civic tools and data experiences that reduce
research time and bring transparency closer to everyday people.

## What we do 🧭

Our work centers on civic information, community engagement, and scalable
technology. We focus on:

- Verified public data and source traceability.
- Interactive experiences that help people learn quickly.
- Creator partnerships that expand reach with high-quality content.
- Infrastructure designed for scale, reliability, and security.

## Principles 📌

Our standards guide every release and partnership we approve.

- Neutrality and factual accuracy.
- Transparency about sources and methods.
- Respect for privacy and responsible data use.
- Accessibility for young, first-time voters and broader audiences.

## Partnership model 🤝

We collaborate with creators and allies who care about civic impact. Our model
is built to reward shared growth.

- Creators get professional tools that improve live engagement.
- The platform grows through real-time use and audience trust.
- Revenue sharing applies when partnerships generate income.

## Current focus 📍

We are preparing for the April 2026 presidential election in Peru while
refining our product with early partner feedback and community insights.
